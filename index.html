Questions

Frequently asked questions

How quickly can you respond to an incident?

Our operations room is staffed 24/7, and mobile units are dispatched the moment an alarm or alert comes in — typical response time is under 15 minutes in our coverage area.

Do I need a long-term contract?

No. We offer flexible terms including short-term and event-based coverage, alongside standard ongoing contracts for sites that want ongoing protection.

Can I get cameras without hiring guards, or guards without cameras?

Yes — the two services are independent. Many clients start with one and add the other later; they're designed to integrate if you want both.

How is footage stored, and can I access it remotely?

Footage is stored securely in the cloud with a defined retention window, and you can view live or recorded footage remotely from your phone or computer.

Are your guards licensed and insured?

Yes — every guard is licensed, background-checked and covered under our insurance, so you carry none of the liability.
